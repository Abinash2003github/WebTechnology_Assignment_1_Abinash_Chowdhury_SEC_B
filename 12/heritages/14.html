Shimogamo Shrine

Shimogamo Shrine is a historic Shinto shrine located in Kyoto, Japan. Founded over 1,200 years ago, it is one of the oldest and most important Shinto shrines in the city. The shrine is dedicated to the deity of the lower Kamo river and is known for its tranquil natural setting, surrounded by lush greenery and ancient trees. Shimogamo Shrine, along with Kamigamo Shrine, forms the Kamo Shrines, which have been central to Kyoto's spiritual and cultural life for centuries. The shrine's picturesque grounds and seasonal festivals attract numerous visitors each year.
