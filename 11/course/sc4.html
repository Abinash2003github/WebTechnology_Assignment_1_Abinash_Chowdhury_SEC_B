Bachelor of Science in Chemical Engineering

Course Description

A Bachelor of Science in Chemical Engineering is a four-year undergraduate degree designed to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of chemical engineering principles. This program typically covers areas such as:

  • Process Engineering: The design and optimization of chemical processes.
  • Thermodynamics: The principles governing heat and energy transfer in chemical systems.
  • Reaction Engineering: The study of chemical reactions and reactor design.
  • Fluid Mechanics: The behavior of fluids in motion and at rest in chemical processes.
  • Materials Science: The properties and applications of materials in chemical engineering.
  • Heat Transfer: Methods of transferring heat within chemical processes.
  • Mass Transfer: The movement of substances in chemical processes.
  • Process Control: Techniques for controlling and automating chemical processes.
