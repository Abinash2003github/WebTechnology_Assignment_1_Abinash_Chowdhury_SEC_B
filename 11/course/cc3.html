Bachelor of Business Studies (BBS)

Course Description

The Bachelor of Business Studies (BBS) is a four-year undergraduate degree designed to provide students with a solid foundation in business principles and practices. This program typically covers various aspects of business management, including:

  • Principles of Management: Fundamental concepts and techniques in managing organizations.
  • Marketing Management: Strategies and practices for effective marketing and customer engagement.
  • Financial Accounting: Basics of accounting, financial statements, and reporting.
  • Human Resource Management: Recruitment, employee development, and organizational behavior.
  • Business Law: Legal aspects related to business operations and corporate governance.
  • Operations Management: Management of production, operations, and supply chain processes.
  • Strategic Management: Planning and implementing business strategies for competitive advantage.
  • Entrepreneurship: Starting and managing new business ventures, innovation, and business planning.
